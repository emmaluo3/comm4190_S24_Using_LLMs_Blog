Blog Post 1: LLMs and Gen Z Slang

Modern Gen Z-ers are defined by the slang and lingo that they’ve created and use in everyday life. This new generation has their own definition of ‘cool’, and it starts off with the topics they speak about and how they talk. In this post I will explore if being ‘Gen Z Cool’ can be learned through LLMs.
